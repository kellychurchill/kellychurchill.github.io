Google site search functionality can be added to any site without the paid service. The paid service only speeds up the indexing of the site's content so that when the site search is performed, it returns Google indexed results. A client can choose to install site search and pay in terms of time instead of money and wait for Google to index the site to have their content show up when the site search is performed.
