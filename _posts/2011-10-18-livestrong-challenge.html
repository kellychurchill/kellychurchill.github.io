I really enjoyed participating in my first LIVESTRONG Challenge.

According to the LIVESTRONG Foundation, there were just over 4,300 riders, walkers and runners, and 700 volunteers that participated in the Challenge this weekend and raised $2.4 million toward the fight against cancer.

I raised over $500 and earned a cool cap.
